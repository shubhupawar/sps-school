Welcome to Sagar Public School

Sagar Public School (SPS) started its journey in 2001 with its first branch at Saket Nagar Bhopal. In the last 17 years, SPS has grown by leaps and bounds to take its place among the top schools of central India. With four branches, and an enrollment of over 7000 students, SPS also enjoys the distinction of being one of the largest schools in central India. SPS, one of the best School in Bhopal, has a vibrant atmosphere for proper teaching and learning and a faculty rich in talent and experience.
